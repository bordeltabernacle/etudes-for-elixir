    etudes v0.0.1 Teeth

    Functions to ascertain the health of teeth. Dentists check the health of your gums by checking the depth of the “pockets” at six different locations around each of your 32 teeth. The depth is measured in millimeters. If any of the depths is greater than or equal to four millimeters, that tooth needs attention.
