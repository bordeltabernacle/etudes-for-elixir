    Summary

    Functions

    area(shape, a, b)

    Calculates the area of a shape, given the shape and two of the dimensions as a tuple. Returns the productof its arguments for a rectangle, one half the product of the arguments for a triangle, and :math.pi times the product of the arguments for an ellipse. Any invalid data returns zero
